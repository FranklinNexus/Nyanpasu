# Privacy Policy for Nyanpasu Wallpaper

**Last Updated**: January 22, 2026

## Overview

Nyanpasu Wallpaper ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our data practices.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal user data.**

### What We Access

1. **Internet Connection**
   - **Purpose**: Download wallpaper images from public APIs (Lolicon API/Pixiv)
   - **Data**: No personal information is transmitted
   - **Storage**: Images are saved locally on your device only

2. **Local Storage**
   - **Purpose**: Store downloaded wallpapers and app preferences
   - **Location**: Private app directory (`/data/data/com.example.acgwallpaper/`)
   - **Access**: Only accessible by the App, never shared

3. **Wallpaper Permission**
   - **Purpose**: Apply images as your device wallpaper
   - **Scope**: Standard Android wallpaper API only

## Data Storage

All data is stored **locally on your device**:

### Stored Data
- Downloaded wallpaper images (PNG format)
- App preferences (tags, schedule settings, dual-mode state)
- Wallpaper history (last 5 wallpapers for undo feature)

### Data Retention
- **Wallpapers**: Stored until you uninstall the app or manually clear data
- **History**: Automatically deleted after 7 days
- **Preferences**: Retained until app uninstall

### Data Deletion
To delete all app data:
1. **Android Settings** ‚Üí **Apps** ‚Üí **Nyanpasu**
2. **Storage** ‚Üí **Clear Data**

Or simply uninstall the app.

## Permissions Explained

### Required Permissions

1. **INTERNET**
   - **Why**: Download wallpaper images
   - **When**: Only when you tap "Refresh" or enable Auto-Update

2. **SET_WALLPAPER**
   - **Why**: Apply images as wallpaper
   - **When**: Only when you toggle Home/Lock buttons

### Optional Permissions

3. **WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE** (Android 9 and below)
   - **Why**: Save wallpapers to Gallery when you tap "Save"
   - **When**: Only when you manually tap the Save button
   - **Note**: On Android 10+, we use Scoped Storage (no permission needed)

4. **RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED**
   - **Why**: Resume scheduled wallpaper updates after device restart
   - **When**: Only if you enable Auto-Update

## Children's Privacy
